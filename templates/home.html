About

A college project named FindMySpot created by Mayank Dhangar.

FindMySpot is a smart vehicle parking management system built to simplify urban parking chaos. Whether you're a daily commuter or a casual driver, the app helps you locate nearby parking slots, pre-book them in real-time, and enjoy a seamless, contactless parking experience.

The system features two dashboards:

  • User Dashboard: Allows users to browse and book parking lots, view available spots, manage bookings, track parking history, and view the summary. It’s clean, mobile-friendly, and highly intuitive.
  • Admin Dashboard: Enables administrators to manage parking lots and spots, view user bookings, monitor space availability, update parking lots, and analyze overall usage and revenue through an interactive UI.

With a focus on usability, responsiveness, and real-time updates, FindMySpot is ideal for smart city initiatives and modern campus or commercial parking solutions. Built using Flask, Bootstrap, and SQLAlchemy, it delivers both functionality and scalability.
